About Me

I’m a first-year master’s student at UC San Diego, specializing in Machine Learning and Data Science within the Electrical and Computer Engineering department. My data science journey started at IIT Bombay, where I earned a Bachelor of Technology degree and completed a minor in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, sparking my passion for solving real-world problems through technology.

Professionally, I spent two years as a Data Scientist at Walmart Global Tech, where I developed innovative models to elevate customer and associate experiences. These models provided actionable insights that drove impactful business decisions, sharpening my expertise in applied machine learning and data-driven problem-solving.

When I’m not immersed in code or algorithms, you’ll often find me pursuing my love for sports, fitness, and outdoor adventures. Whether it’s hitting the gym, leading a cricket match, hiking scenic trails, or simply catching up with friends, I thrive on staying active and connected.
